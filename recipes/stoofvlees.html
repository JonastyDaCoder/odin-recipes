Stoofvlees with fries

A plate of stoofvlees with fries

For the beef, Jeroen prefers the nerve piece from the shoulder, very budget-friendly beef that is ideal for a tasty stew. With the right beer, some herbs, some syrup and a portion of patience, you can put this winner on the table in no time. Enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • klontje boter
  • halve kilo rundsvlees
  • peper
  • snuifje zout
  • 1 fles bruin bier Sint Bernardus abt 12
  • 1 blaadje laurier
  • ...

Steps

  1. Peel the onions and chop them into not too fine pieces.
